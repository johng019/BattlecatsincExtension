WE ARE THE BATTLECATS

You won't find a better deal anywhere else, guaranteed.

BattleCats is an organization founded in 2020 built upon providing the highest quality service and parts at reasonable price by a small group of individuals at Valencia College in Orlando, Florida. The college team that founded it had seen a gap in the computer parts market that needed to be filled. With so many options available for the average consumer as well as the seasoned pro, there needed to be a place where anyone could get access to what they needed quickly and without headache.

From these principles, BattleCats Inc. was created, and their platform solidified with giving a simple and crisp experience to all consumers in the computer market. These founders continue to work diligently even after their company grew to the e-commerce giant that it is today. As our world continues to evolve, so will we. We want to continue to meet our consumers ever changing expectations and give the highest quality experience we can. This is why we hire and cultivate talented individuals with a passion with computers and customer service to be able to help our consumers with any of their needs. From repairs to simple questions, our certified staff is more than happy to help at every turn.

Our greatest achievement is being able to give back to the people by donating computers and parts to schools and small businesses. By doing so, we’re able to provide for future generations to grow and learn how to work with parts and computers to further their careers in the IT field. We might strive for the best prices and parts first, but what truly comes first is taking care of the next generation of builders and engineers. With knowledge in the technological field, the possibilities are endless. In the future we hope to pathe a path of success so that anybody can shape their own future into being a bright one.

We take pride in our company ideals, employees, and products, and hope that you find everything you’re looking for. Being partnered with companies such as Amazon, UPS, Newegg, Microcenter, and much more, our knowledge for technology and pricing is ever expanding. From our highest rated customer service and employee rating to our individual trusted vendors, you will have an amazing experience at every turn on our platform. Even if BattleCats isn’t the site that you choose to buy from, we hope that our simplicity and helpful platform can give you a better understanding of the technical world.
